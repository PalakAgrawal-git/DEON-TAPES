Annotations

1

Sticky top nav. Logo left; primary links (Tapes, Films, About, Career, Press, Quality & Certifications, Markets ▾); language selector + Contact button right. Collapses to a ☰ menu on mobile.

2

Breadcrumb. Home › Quality & Certifications, with the page tagline.

3

Hero. Eyebrow label, large H1 (“Manufactured to recognised quality standards”), and a one-line intro naming the standards (ISO 9001 / 45001 / 14001, UL, BIS). No hero image in the reference — text only.

4

Certification card grid. One card per standard — ISO 9001:2015, ISO 45001:2018, ISO 14001:2015, UL, BIS · IS 7809. Each card: “Certified” badge, standard name, scope line, “Certificate available on request”. 3-up on desktop, 1-up on mobile.

5

Documentation block. “Documented & audit-ready” — three items: Datasheets (per product), Test reports (per batch), Samples (on request).

6

CTA band. “Need documentation?” with a primary Request Documents button and a secondary Knowledge Center link.

7

Mega-footer. Company blurb + Contact CTA, then link columns (Products, Markets, Company, Resources) and a legal row (Legal, Privacy, Terms, Accessibility, Sitemap) + “It’s Power-Strong”.
