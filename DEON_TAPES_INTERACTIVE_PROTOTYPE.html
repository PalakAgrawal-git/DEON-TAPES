Current page: Home
How to use: Only the items in the top navigation bar (Home, About, Infrastructure, etc.) are clickable in this prototype — click them to move between pages, exactly like real tabs on a website. All other buttons and cards (e.g. "Contact Us", "View Details") are shown as static placeholders for now, just to indicate where they will sit and what they will say once the site is built.

Typical user journey

  1. User lands on Home.
  2. Clicks the Products tab in the nav bar → moves to Products Hub.
  3. Browses product cards (static for now) → would open a Product Detail page.
  4. Clicks the Contact tab → moves to Contact Us page to send an enquiry.
  5. At any point, the same nav bar lets the user jump to About, Infrastructure, Quality & Certs, or Blog.
