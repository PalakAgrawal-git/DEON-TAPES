Typical user journey
  1. User lands on Home.
  2. Clicks the Products tab in the nav bar → moves to Products Hub.
  3. Browses product cards → opens a Product Detail page.
  4. Clicks the Contact tab → moves to Contact Us page to send an enquiry.
  5. At any point, the nav bar lets the user jump to About, Infrastructure, Quality & Certs, or Blog.
